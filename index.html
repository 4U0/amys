FAQ

How does the middleman process work?

Typical flow:

  1. Initiation: Buyer & Seller contact me.
  2. Agreement: Confirm deal details & fee.
  3. Buyer Deposit: Buyer sends agreed funds/assets + MM Fee (in crypto) to my address.
  4. Confirmation: I confirm receipt.
  5. Seller Action: Seller delivers goods/service.
  6. Buyer Confirmation: Buyer confirms receipt & okays release.
  7. Release: I send funds/assets to Seller.
  8. Completion: Deal done!
